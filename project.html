Welcome to the Dice Game! Here's how you can play:

  • Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll a pair of dice for both the player and the computer.
  • The scores for each roll are calculated and displayed below the dice.
  • After three rolls, the total scores for both the player and the computer are displayed.
  • The player with the highest total score wins the game!
  • Click the "Reset Game" button to start a new game.

The scoring for the game works as follows:

  • If any of the dice show a 1, the score for that round is 0. For example, if you roll a 6 and 1, your score is 0.
  • If both dice show the same number, the score is the sum of the dice values times 2. For example, if you roll two 5s, your score is (5+5) * 2 = 20.
  • Otherwise, the score is the sum of the dice values. For example, if you roll a 3 and 2, your score is 3 + 2 = 5.
